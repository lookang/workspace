Magnetic Field Demo

The Magnetic Field Demo shows the field of a bar magnet, a coil, and Earth. It has a movable compass that reports the field magnitude (strength) and a grid of magnetic needles that show the field direction. The magnetic field is modeled using magnetic dipoles underneath the image. Why is it reasonable to model a magnet using multiple dipoles? How may dipoles are there in an actual bar magnet?
